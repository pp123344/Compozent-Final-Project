How To Use

Welcome to the Movie Recommendation System! Here’s how To Use:

1. Select Genres: Choose your favorite genres from a variety of options such as Action, Comedy, Drama, and more.

2. Select Your Preference: Choose your preference for recommendations—either based on high ratings or recent releases.

3. Get Recommendations: Once you've made your selections, hit "Get Recommendations" and the system will show you the top 10 movies based on your criteria.

Explore and Enjoy the latest movies up to 2024 with personalized recommendations!
